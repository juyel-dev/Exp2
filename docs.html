Documentation & Learning

Binaural beats are an auditory illusion where two slightly different frequencies are played in each ear, creating a perceived "beat" in the brain.

Benefits:

  • Relaxation (4-8 Hz)
  • Focus (10-20 Hz)
  • Sleep (1-4 Hz)
  • Meditation (8-12 Hz)

How to Use:

1. Wear headphones. 2. Select preset or adjust frequencies. 3. Play and relax.
