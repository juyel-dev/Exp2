Learn About Binaural Beats

What are Binaural Beats?

Binaural beats occur when two slightly different frequencies are played in each ear, creating a perceived beat in the brain. Used for relaxation, focus, meditation.

How to Use This App

1. Wear headphones. 2. Select frequencies or preset. 3. Play and relax. Offline support available via PWA install.
